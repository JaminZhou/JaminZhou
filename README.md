## Current focus

- Shipping [GarlicBird](https://jaminzhou.com/garlicbird/), an iOS shopping calculator for comparing unit prices across package sizes, currencies, coupons, deposits, and discounts.
- Shipping [Rouse](https://jaminzhou.com/rouse/), a macOS utility for keeping your Mac awake during AI agents, long downloads, exports, presentations, and other long-running work.
- Building focused apps and workflow utilities for Apple platforms.
- Keeping products clear, fast, and useful without turning them into control panels.

## Featured projects

### GarlicBird

GarlicBird is a focused iPhone shopping helper for calculating and comparing real unit prices when package sizes, currencies, coupons, deposits, and full-reduction discounts make the shelf price hard to trust.

- [Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/garlicbird/id6762044720)
- [Visit the product page](https://jaminzhou.com/garlicbird/)
- [Support](https://jaminzhou.com/garlicbird/support/) · [Privacy](https://jaminzhou.com/garlicbird/privacy/)

### Rouse

Rouse keeps your Mac awake with a fast menu bar toggle, a calm dashboard for timed sessions, display sleep, and battery-aware controls, plus an official CLI for Terminal, scripts, and local automation.

- [Download on the Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/rouse-stay-awake/id6760773101?mt=12)
